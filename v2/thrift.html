ThriftEasy

  • MongoDB
  • Express
  • React
  • Node.js
  • Tailwind CSS

It's not just a search engine; it's an experience that uses the MERN stack to make the search for second-hand treasures a visually delightful and simple experience.

This dynamic creation integrates JWT, Firebase, and Google OAuth to ensure secure and frictionless user access. The backend, powered by Express and Node.js, orchestrates a REST API, while the frontend emerges as a visual masterpiece with React and Tailwind CSS. In the realm of real-world CRUD operations, developed by MongoDB, users effortlessly create, read, update, and delete their accounts. Security, seamless functionality, and aesthetic design converge to redefine the landscape of online second-hand shopping exploration.
